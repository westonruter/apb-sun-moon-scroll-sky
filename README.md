# APB Sun & Moon Scroll Sky

This plugin was generated by the [AI‑Powered Plugin Builder for WordPress](https://hackathon.cloudfest.com/project/ai-powered-plugin-builder-for-wordpress/) project at the 2026 [CloudFest Hackathon](https://hackathon.cloudfest.com/). See the [project website](https://wpaipluginbuilder.org/).  

The following prompt was used:

> Place a sun shining from the bottom-right corner of the screen (fully in the viewport) when the user is scrolled at the top of the page. This is the sunrise state. Let the background be a sunrise gradient. Then as the user scrolls down the page, use scroll-driven animations to move the sun's fixed position vertically to the right middle of the page, in a straight line along the right edge. When scrolled to 50% it is high noon, and the sun should be in the right edge of the page, half-way down. Gradually transition the sunrise gradient to a sky blue when approaching 15% scroll. Let it be blue until reaching 75%, at which point transition to sunset gradient. When scrolled to 90%, the sun moves off-screen as it goes off the page at the bottom right. When scrolling past 90%, the page enters dark mode. All text on the page (including links, headings, etc) must transition to white and the background should transition/fade from sunset gradient to a star field. At this point, a moon moves in from the top right. Never abruptly change the sky color. Always fade. Also, the sky must not obscure the content of the page (it must appear behind, as the background). If scroll-driven animations aren't supported, then do nothing.
